Apple Inc.

About Apple

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Apple designs, manufactures, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Apple's most popular products include the iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch.

History

Apple was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976. The company initially focused on the development and sale of personal computers. Over the years, Apple has expanded its product lineup to include various consumer electronics, software, and services.

Products

Apple's product lineup includes:

  • iPhone
  • iPad
  • Mac
  • Apple Watch
  • Apple TV
